About Our Founder

Johnathan Magna

Founder & CEO

With over 25 years of experience in the construction industry, Johnathan Magna established Magna Infra in 2005 with a vision to redefine construction excellence. His leadership has guided the company through numerous successful projects, earning a reputation for quality, reliability, and innovation.

Under his guidance, Magna Infra has grown from a small local contractor to a respected name in commercial and residential construction, known for completing projects on time and within budget while maintaining the highest safety standards.

"We don't just build structures; we create landmarks that stand the test of time and serve communities for generations."
